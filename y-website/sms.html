Don't have time to call and need to drop a message ASAP? Now you can.

International SMS messaging with Yolla is a fast and cheap way to communicate with your friends and family when you are abroad. The SMS rate is only $0.15 per message.

Sending an international SMS through Yolla is as simple as sending a regular SMS.

But at first you need to install the app. Click the store buttons below or scan the QR code with your camera to download the app and get a top-up bonus:

After getting Yolla you can start sending international SMS:

  • Step 1. Open Yolla and go to contacts

  • Step 2. Open a contact you would like to send a message to & tap “SMS” button

  • Step 3. Write a message and tap the “Send” icon
